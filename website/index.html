About Us

Armed with clippers, scissors, and enough military precision to make your hair stand at attention, our crew of veteran barbers brings discipline and sharp fades to the City by the Sea.

Newport may be less than 12 square miles, but it somehow has over 30 places to get your hair cut. That's a lot of competition—and even more questionable bangs. But right in the middle of Thames Street, Hair Apparent has carved out its own niche: a barbershop where clean lines meet coastal vibes, and the only thing smoother than our fades is the banter.

Our barbers are military vets, which means every cut is executed with focus, pride, and absolutely zero tolerance for uneven sideburns. The atmosphere? Relaxed. The jokes? Free. The haircuts? So sharp they could probably pass inspection.

Whether you're a sailor, a student, a local legend, or a tourist who made one bad decision with a pair of kitchen scissors—we've got you.

At Hair Apparent, we don't just cut hair… we raise morale.
